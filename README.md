# Bunny & Lola Photobooth

> A vintage-inspired virtual photobooth with Melbourne soul and storytelling at its heart.

## The Story

My Lola was mad at me. So her bunny made this.

Bunny & Lola is a virtual photobooth that captures more than just photos—it captures moments, stories, and the unique energy of the cities we love. Born in Melbourne, built with nostalgia, and designed for connection.

---

## Features

### Camera & Photo Capture
- **Solo Mode** – Take photos alone, design your strip, save your memories
- **Duo Mode** – Create a room, share a code, take photos with friends
- Countdown timer (3, 2, 1, cheese)
- Choose between 3 or 4 photos per strip
- Preview photos before designing

### Vintage Photostrip Design
- Black and white with sepia filter – Authentic vintage feel
- Film grain texture – Analog warmth
- Film strip borders – Classic photobooth aesthetics
- Story captions – Tell a mini story across your frames
- Stickers – Add personality with bunny, hearts, stars, flowers, and more

### City Vibes
Each city changes the mood, paper color, and atmosphere:

**Australian Cities**
- **Melbourne** – Laneway vibes, street art, coffee culture
- **Sydney** – Beach vibes, sunny, golden hour
- **Brisbane** – Tropical, lush green, warm colors
- **Perth** – Sunset vibes, Indian ocean blues
- **Adelaide** – Wine country, rolling hills, elegant

**International Cities**
- **New York** – Urban, neon lights, edgy
- **Paris** – Romantic, Eiffel Tower, vintage chic
- **Tokyo** – Cherry blossoms, neon pop, kawaii
- **London** – Classic, phone booths, rainy aesthetic
- **Santorini** – White and blue, Mediterranean

**Seasonal Themes**
- **Summer** – Beach, sunshine, bright colors
- **Winter** – Snow, cozy, cool blues
- **Autumn** – Warm oranges, falling leaves
- **Spring** – Pastels, flowers, fresh

### Gallery & Print
- Save photostrips to local gallery
- Download high-quality vintage PNG strips
- Printable format with city-specific styling
- Persistent storage in browser

## Tech Stack

### Frontend
- **React** with TypeScript – Type-safe components
- **Tailwind CSS** – Custom editorial styling
- **Framer Motion** – Smooth animations
- **React Webcam** – Camera access
- **html2canvas** – Photostrip rendering
- **React Router** – Navigation

### Backend (Optional)
- **Netlify Functions** – Serverless API endpoints
- **Supabase** – Database and storage
- **Socket.IO** – Real-time duo mode

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Node.js (v18 or higher)
- npm or yarn
